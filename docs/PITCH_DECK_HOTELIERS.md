# STAYLO — Hotelier Pitch Deck (10 slides, EN)

Audience: independent Thai hoteliers (hotels, guesthouses, hostels, resorts).
Goal: get them to register at staylo.app/submit in 15 minutes.

Each slide below has:
- **Title** — the headline on screen
- **Body** — what's written on the slide (keep it tight, max 3 bullets)
- **Visual** — image suggestion
- **Spoken** — what to say live if presenting (skip if it's a leave-behind PDF)

---

## Slide 1 · Hook

**Title:** You make the bed. Booking takes 22%.

**Subtitle:** A booking platform built by hoteliers, for hoteliers.

**Visual:** Split screen — left: a hotelier polishing a room at sunrise · right: a glass tower with the Booking.com logo and dollar signs floating away.

**Spoken:**
> "Quick question. On every $100 a guest pays you through Booking.com — how much do you actually keep? Most of you say 78. Some of you say 75. The truth is closer to 70 once you count the hidden fees. We think this is broken. So we built something different."

## Design notes (brand consistency)

- **Palette**: STAYLO orange `#FF6B00` → magenta `#E91E63` gradient (the one in your logo). Use sparingly — once per slide for the headline accent. Background mostly cream `#FFFDF8` or white.
- **Type**: Inter (already in your stack), bold for titles, regular for body. Avoid italics.
- **Photos**: real Thai hotelier shots > stock photos. Even a slightly imperfect real photo beats a polished stock image because it signals authenticity.
- **Don't**: use crypto-bro neon, finance-bro charts, or generic "team huddle" stock shots. Stay grounded.

---

## Localisation notes

When you translate to Thai for the Thai audience:
- Keep the **numbers in USD** (Thai hoteliers think in USD for OTA commission discussions — it's the lingua franca)
- Translate **only the headers and key bullets** — leave the comparison table numbers untranslated
- Add a **footer line in Thai** on slide 10: "พูดคุยกับเดวิดได้โดยตรงทาง WhatsApp"
- Avoid translating "STAYLO" — keep the brand in Latin script
