# When Thai · ภาษาไทย Phonetic (stress in CAPS)
P1 Greeting + wai สวัสดีครับ คุณศศิวิมล ขอบคุณมากครับที่สละเวลา
Sa-WAT-dee KRAP, Khun Sa-si-WI-mol. KHOP-khun MAAK KRAP tee SA-la WE-la.
"Good afternoon Khun Sasiwimol, thank you for your time."

Dtorn-NEE Booking.com gep SIP-BPAET peh-SENT. Ngern HOK-JOOT-SAAM LAAN DOL-laa OHK jaak Gao-Pa-NGAN took PEE krap.
"Booking takes 18%. $6.3M leaves Koh Phangan every year."
P3 Min 3-4 — coop กับ Staylo คุณเป็นเจ้าของแพลตฟอร์มเองครับ หนึ่งโรงแรม หนึ่งเสียงโหวต ตลอดไป

Top Objections · Responses
"Is this crypto / risky?"
No. STAYLO is a booking platform. The token is a loyalty reward, ignorable. The hotelier never touches crypto if they don't want to.
"Can I buy more than 1 share?"
Yes — up to 10 max per property at $1,000 ($10K max). Beyond 10 = wait for World round at $1,500. 1 property = 1 vote regardless of share count.
"What about restaurants and tours?"
Phase 2 (F&B · M12, $TASTE token). Phase 3 (Tours · M24, $TOUR). Phase 1 hoteliers get priority access to every future Alpha round, before public open.
"What if STAYLO fails? I lose $1,000?"
Honest: yes — like any early-stage. Mitigation: launch only at 500 hotels committed. No 500 = no launch = full refund.
"Do I have to leave Booking?"
No. STAYLO is a parallel channel. Use both. Shift bookings over time as STAYLO grows.
"When do I see money back?"
Commission savings: day 1 of launch. Dividends: year 2 (after coop reserves funded).
"Who runs STAYLO? Is it Thai?"
Founder David Deveaux (FR/TH). Cooperative legal entity becomes Thai once 500 hotels join. Hoteliers govern — not David.
