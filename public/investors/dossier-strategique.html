1 — Vision

STAYLO becomes the operating economy of independent hospitality. Not a "better Booking.com" — a fundamentally different model where the hotels using the platform also own it, govern it, and capture the upside. The cooperative pivots the value flow: instead of $6.3M/year leaving Koh Phangan for OTA shareholders in Amsterdam and New York, it stays in the local economy and accrues to the hoteliers who generated it. Same principle, scaled: Thailand → SE Asia → EU → LATAM.

2 — Why now

  • OTA fatigue: 17-25% commissions feel like a tax. Hotels want exit, not better integration.
  • Crypto / Bitcoin tooling mature: Lightning Network production-ready · Solana SPL-2022 standard · stable infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel, Stripe).
  • Cooperative renaissance: conscious traveler demographic doubling YoY · "ethical hospitality" search demand up 4× since 2024.
  • Thai timing: 2026-2027 Thailand pivots to high-value tourism · digital nomad visas · regulatory openness to Web3 (BoT sandbox).
  • Personal-edge: founder operates a Koh Phangan hotel directly — built STAYLO Ship FOR his own staff. Product-market fit observed live.

3 — Go-to-market

Beachhead: Koh Phangan (420 active hotels) · founder is local · ambassador (Sasiwimol) leads community trust building · KP Founding Partner round at $1,000 / share creates 300+ hotelier-owners before launch · 🎄 X-Mas 2026 launch trigger (400-500 KP signed OR 500 shares sold).

Expansion sequence: Phase 2 (2027) — Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai, Krabi. Phase 3 (2028) — Cambodia, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia. Phase 4-5 (2029-2031) — Japan, Korea, India, EU, Americas. Each new region opens with an in-region cooperative cohort (50-100 founding hotels) before public rollout.
