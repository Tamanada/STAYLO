1 — Token at a glance

$STAY is the utility + governance token of the STAYLO cooperative. It rewards hoteliers and guests for actions that grow the network, pays for premium platform features, and represents non-financial voting weight where applicable. 10 billion fixed supply, no inflation, no new minting (90% supermajority required to even discuss it). Bitcoin-style halving every 4 years on all emissions makes early participation the most valuable.

4 — Spend & utility

  • 5% commission discount when hoteliers pay platform fee in $STAY (vs 10% cash). 50% of tokens used are burned — opt-in deflationary mechanism.
  • Profile boosts for guests (verified badge, priority on partner hotel comp lists).
  • DAO proposal stake (small refundable deposit to submit a vote).
  • Future: bridge incentives, partner co-marketing pools, deferred listing on top tier exchanges.

5 — Hold & governance

Governance follows the cooperative principle: 1 property = 1 vote for hoteliers (sybil-proof — independent of share or token balance). 1 share = 1 vote for investors except Private Strategic (NO VOTE). $STAY balance does NOT confer voting power — but does serve as the de-facto loyalty layer for guests across the network (earn at one hotel, spend at all).

6 — Token security

  • Standard: SPL Token-2022 (Solana). Audit before TGE — budget reserved (Halborn / Trail of Bits / OpenZeppelin shortlist).
  • Mint authority: revoked at TGE. No new $STAY ever — except a 1%/year emergency cap on the engagement pool, gated by 90% DAO vote.
  • Freeze authority: revoked. Censorship-resistant.
  • Multisig: treasury controlled by 5-of-7 multisig (Singapore directors + independent custodian).

7 — Bitcoin-style scarcity narrative

$STAY is engineered as a scarcity-anchored loyalty asset, not a speculative token. Fixed 10B supply + halving every 4 years means each unit becomes 2× harder to earn over time. Early hoteliers (and guests) capture the highest emission rates — a real incentive to onboard during Phase 0/1, not Phase 5. The optional burn mechanism (5% commission in $STAY → 50% burned) compounds the scarcity narrative without dependency on price action.
